About the Game

What is our purpose?

The purpose of our game is to increase awareness for the issue of racial privilege and to demonstrate that some may have less opportunity due to circumstances that they are not in control of. Privilege is real, which is the main point that we’re trying to bring across.

What is Privilege?

Privilege is a special right or advantage available only to a particular person or group of people. More specifically, our game targets the issue of racial privilege in our society today.

When will privilege end?

Privilege will only end when the privileged can empathize with those that don’t have the same opportunities and ability to advocate for themselves.

Why is privilege important?

It is important to recognize that the social issues from our society stems from our history of oppressing specific groups of people through Linnaeus' idea of "scientific racism".
